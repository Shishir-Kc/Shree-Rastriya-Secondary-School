Shree Rastriya Mavi Secondary School

About Us

Established with a vision to provide quality education to all, Shree Rastriya Mavi Secondary School has been a beacon of learning and excellence in our community. Our institution caters to students from Pre-Primary to Grade 12, offering a nurturing environment that promotes academic, cultural, and personal growth.

With a dedicated team of highly qualified educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to foster a love for learning while preparing students for the challenges of the future. We believe in holistic development, ensuring our students excel academically, socially, and ethically.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values, enabling them to contribute positively to society and achieve their fullest potential.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that inspires a lifelong love of learning and cultivates responsible global citizens.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity and honesty in all endeavors
  • Respect for diversity and inclusivity
  • Commitment to academic excellence
  • Fostering creativity and innovation
  • Encouraging teamwork and collaboration
